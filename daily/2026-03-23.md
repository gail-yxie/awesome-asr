# ASR & Speech Language Daily Update — 2026-03-23

## Summary

Today’s model releases highlight a strong trend toward localized inference and the expansion of ASR capabilities for low-resource languages like Karakalpak and Belarusian. Significant emphasis is being placed on hardware-specific optimization for the Apple ecosystem, as seen in the release of Belarusian Conformer variants for the MLX framework and a CoreML conversion of NVIDIA’s Nemotron-Speech 600M model. The continued use of parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT), particularly LoRA for Whisper large-v3, demonstrates the primary method for adapting state-of-the-art backbones to niche linguistic domains. Collectively, these developments reflect a shift from general-purpose model scaling toward the practical deployment of specialized, high-performance speech engines on edge devices.
