# ASR & Speech Language Daily Update — 2026-03-07

## Summary

Today’s releases highlight a strong push toward edge-optimized and architecturally diverse ASR, led by the community integration of the Qwen3-ASR (0.6B) model and its CoreML counterpart for on-device inference. Emerging experimentation with the Granite architecture for speech (granite-4.0-1b-speech) and the application of NVIDIA’s Parakeet framework for Uzbek STT indicates a diversification of backbones beyond standard Whisper-based pipelines. Furthermore, specialized fine-tuning of Whisper for low-resource languages like Myanmar and Khmer, as well as niche domains like Quebecois rap, continues to address performance gaps in regional and dialectal speech. Finally, the release of Moonshine-streaming in ONNX format underscores the persistent industry demand for high-performance, low-latency streaming solutions tailored for cross-platform hardware deployment.
