# ASR & Speech Language Daily Update — 2026-04-21

## Summary

Today’s releases underscore a significant push toward efficient deployment and on-device inference, highlighted by the CoreML export of NVIDIA’s Nemotron-Speech-0.6B and GGUF quantization of OmniASR-LLM. These developments, alongside the ONNX export of SenseVoiceSmall and CTranslate2 versions of Whisper-Turbo, reflect a clear trend toward optimizing compact speech language models for low-latency, streaming applications. Furthermore, localized and domain-specific fine-tuning remains a core focus, with new practitioners releasing targeted models for Nepali ASR, radiology-specific dictation, and dysarthric speech recognition via the UASpeech dataset. Collectively, these updates demonstrate that the field is rapidly moving to bridge the gap between high-parameter speech research and specialized, production-ready edge deployment.
