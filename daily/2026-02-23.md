# ASR & Speech Language Daily Update — 2026-02-23

## Summary

Today’s developments center on model optimization for edge deployment and specialized transcription tasks, notably with the CoreML adaptation of NVIDIA’s Parakeet-TDT architecture for high-efficiency on-device ASR. Whisper remains the dominant foundation for community fine-tuning, with new variants like `ipa-whisper-medium` targeting International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) transcription and various regional low-resource language refinements. The release of Voxtral-Mini-4B further signals the ongoing research trend of merging speech capabilities with small language models (SLMs) to create more integrated, multimodal conversational agents. Collectively, these releases reflect a dual priority in the field: porting high-performance transducer-based models to local hardware while pushing the boundaries of phonetic and bilingual accuracy through targeted fine-tuning.
